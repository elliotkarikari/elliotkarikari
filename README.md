### Hi there 👋

I am a data scientist and spatial analyst working at the intersection of geospatial analytics, public health, mobility, evaluation, and decision-support systems.

My work focuses on turning complex datasets into practical evidence: movement behavior, health and nutrition data, social vulnerability, environmental exposure, and public-sector decision-making.

## Selected work

### Mobility and route-choice analysis
Analyzed large-scale connected-car GPS data to understand driver navigation behavior across the UK, using spatial metrics, clustering, and exploratory analysis across more than 1 million journeys.

### Retail health and nutrition analytics
Built data workflows for product classification, standardization, and health/nutrition analysis, including work with large product datasets and category-level measurement.

### Spatial inequality and access analysis
Developed district-level deprivation and healthcare-access analysis in Ghana using census data, boundaries, and point-of-interest datasets.

### Evaluation and public-good analytics
Designed survey instruments, scoring approaches, and reporting frameworks for education, climate, and social-impact projects.
